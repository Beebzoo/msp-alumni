The Deal

Here's what we offer, what we ask, and what we promise.

We Offer

  • A global network of 1,330+ MSP graduates
  • WhatsApp communities in 24+ countries
  • Quarterly newsletter with alumni news
  • Career connections and networking
  • Merch giveaways and exclusive content

We Ask

  • Keep us updated occasionally
  • Respond to one survey per year
  • Tell prospective students if you loved MSP
  • Share your story (optional)

We Promise

  • Respect your privacy always
  • Never spam you

  • Keep communications minimal and valuable

You Control
