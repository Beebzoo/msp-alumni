Thanks for Being Here

The alumni office at MSP has been working hard to build a database so we can follow the inspiring journeys of our graduates around the world. The challenge? A lot of the emails we have are outdated, which means surveys and updates don't reach everyone. That's where you come in — by sharing some basic info, you help us plan better activities, organize events, and keep the community connected.

Your Data, Your Control

Here's exactly what we do — and don't do — with your information

What We Do

  • Use your info to send you relevant updates and newsletters
  • Create anonymous, aggregated statistics for the website (e.g., "462 alumni in the Netherlands")
  • Organize events in cities where alumni are located
  • Connect you with other alumni if you both agree
  • Always ask before featuring you publicly

What We Never Do

  • Share your personal information with third parties
  • Display your name or details publicly without explicit consent
  • Sell or monetize your data in any way
  • Spam you — we send 3-4 emails per year maximum
  • Make it difficult to opt out (just email us anytime)

Want the full details? Our privacy policy explains everything in plain language.
