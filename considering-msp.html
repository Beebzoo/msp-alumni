The Programme

What is MSP?

A 3-year English-taught Bachelor's where you design your own science curriculum

Design Your Own Path
Choose from 150+ courses across biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics, and more. Create a curriculum that matches your interests and goals.
Problem-Based Learning
Learn in small groups with UM's signature PBL method. Develop critical thinking, collaboration, and self-directed learning skills.
Truly International
Join students from 78 nationalities. Study in English in the heart of Europe, with easy access to Germany, Belgium, and beyond.
