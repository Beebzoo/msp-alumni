The Programme

What is the Maastricht Science Programme?

MSP is a 3-year English-taught Bachelor's programme where you design your own science degree. Unlike traditional programmes that lock you into one subject, MSP lets you combine courses across biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics, and neuroscience.

Design Your Path

Choose from 150+ courses to create a curriculum that matches your interests and career goals

Small-Scale Education

Learn in tutorial groups of 12-15 students using Problem-Based Learning (PBL)

Research From Day One

Hands-on lab work and a final thesis project at world-class research institutions
