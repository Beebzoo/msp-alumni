Frequently Asked Questions

How can I update my information in the alumni database?
Send an email to msp-alumni@maastrichtuniversity.nl with your updated details (current location, job, university, etc.). We'll update your record within a few days. You can also respond to our annual survey each September.
How do I join the WhatsApp community?
The WhatsApp community is open to all MSP alumni. Visit our Community page for the join link, or email us if you need assistance. We have a main announcement channel plus 24 country-specific groups.
Can I be featured in a success story?
Yes! We're always looking for alumni willing to share their journey. We feature diverse paths — not just PhDs or prestigious positions. If you're interested, email us and we'll arrange a brief interview. You'll approve the final content before publication.
How is my data used and protected?
We only display aggregated, anonymized statistics publicly. Your individual information is never shared without consent. Read our full Privacy Policy for details on data handling, your rights, and how to request changes or deletion.
I'm a current student — can I access alumni resources?
Absolutely! While the WhatsApp community is for graduates, you can explore this website, attend alumni events, and request connections with alumni in your field of interest. Just email us with what you're looking for and we'll help facilitate introductions.
How can I give back to MSP?
There are several ways: give a guest lecture, mentor current students, participate in recruitment events, or simply share your story to inspire prospective students. Contact us to discuss opportunities that match your interests and availability.
