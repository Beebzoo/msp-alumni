Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

The Maastricht Science Programme (MSP) values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal data for the MSP Alumni Network — a community platform that helps us stay connected with our graduates and demonstrate the impact of our programme.

We are committed to processing your data fairly, transparently, and in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The MSP Alumni Network operates as part of Maastricht University's alumni engagement activities.

Data Controller

Maastricht University

Faculty of Science and Engineering — Maastricht Science Programme

P.O. Box 616, 6200 MD Maastricht, The Netherlands

General privacy inquiries: privacy@maastrichtuniversity.nl

Data Protection Officer: fg@maastrichtuniversity.nl

What Data We Process

We process a limited set of personal data to maintain our alumni community and understand graduate outcomes. This helps us improve the programme and provide prospective students with accurate information about where MSP can lead.

Data from university records

When you graduate, we retain basic information from your student record:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Date of birth and nationality
  • Programme and graduation date

Data you provide

Through our graduation survey or subsequent updates, you may share:

  • Your current location (city/country)
  • Your Master's programme and university
  • Your current role and employer
  • Communication preferences

Publicly available information

To ensure our records remain accurate and to understand graduate outcomes at an aggregate level, we may occasionally review publicly available information on professional networking sites such as LinkedIn. This simply helps us verify that the information we have is correct — for example, confirming which Master's programme an alumnus completed.

Please note: We only look at information you have chosen to make public. We never attempt to access private profiles or scrape data. If you would prefer we do not use this source for your records, simply let us know at msp-alumni@maastrichtuniversity.nl and we will rely solely on information you provide directly.

Why We Process Your Data

We use alumni data for the following purposes:

Purpose What this means
Staying in touch Sending occasional newsletters, event invitations, and programme updates to keep you connected with MSP
Understanding outcomes Analysing where graduates go and what they achieve, so we can improve the programme and demonstrate its value
Supporting prospective students Sharing aggregate statistics (e.g., "X% of alumni pursue PhDs") to help prospective students make informed choices
Facilitating connections Helping alumni connect with each other through our WhatsApp communities and networking opportunities — always with your consent

Legal Basis

We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): Maintaining an alumni community and tracking graduate outcomes are standard activities for a university programme. We have assessed that this processing does not override your rights, particularly because you can opt out at any time and we only use data in ways you would reasonably expect.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR): For optional activities — such as featuring your story on our website, joining WhatsApp groups, or participating in mentorship — we always ask for your explicit permission first.

What Appears on the Public Website

The MSP Alumni Network website displays aggregate statistics only. This includes:

  • Total number of alumni and their geographic distribution (e.g., "445 alumni in the Netherlands")
  • Types of Master's programmes pursued (by category, not individually)
  • Career sectors and PhD statistics (as totals and percentages)
Your individual information is never displayed publicly without your explicit consent. We do not publish names, photos, individual career details, or any personally identifiable information unless you have specifically agreed to be featured as a success story.

Who We Share Data With

We do not sell your data. We may share information only with:

  • Maastricht University central services — The Alumni Office and Marketing & Communications, for coordinated university-wide alumni activities
  • Research partners — Only anonymised, aggregate data for academic research purposes
  • Service providers — Technical providers for website hosting and email services, all based within the EU

We never share your individual contact details with external parties without your permission.

How Long We Keep Your Data

Alumni relationships are lifelong, and understanding long-term outcomes is valuable for programme development. Therefore, we retain alumni data indefinitely unless you ask us to delete it.

If you request deletion, we will remove your personal details promptly. However, certain records must be retained for legal reasons:

Data type Retention Reason
Degree records 30 years minimum Dutch Public Records Act
Contact information Until you request deletion Ongoing relationship
Career outcomes Indefinitely (can be anonymised on request) Longitudinal research
Opt-out records Indefinitely To ensure we respect your preferences

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Restriction — Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — Withdraw any consent you have given, at any time

To exercise any of these rights, simply email msp-alumni@maastrichtuniversity.nl. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data:

  • Access to alumni records is restricted to authorised MSP staff only
  • All connections to our website use secure encryption (HTTPS)
  • Our systems are hosted within the European Union
  • We conduct regular reviews of our security practices

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for basic functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any cookies that collect personal data. We do not share browsing data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify registered alumni by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last revised.

Questions or Complaints

If you have questions about how we handle your data, or if you believe your privacy rights have been affected, please contact us:

  • MSP Alumni Office: msp-alumni@maastrichtuniversity.nl
  • UM Data Protection Officer: fg@maastrichtuniversity.nl

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority:
https://autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl
