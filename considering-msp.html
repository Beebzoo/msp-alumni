The Programme

What is MSP?

A 3-year English-taught Bachelor's where you design your own science curriculum

Design Your Path

Choose from 100+ courses across biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics, and neuroscience to build your unique degree.

Small-Scale Learning

Tutorial groups of 12-15 students using Problem-Based Learning. You're not a number — you're part of a community.

Research Experience

Hands-on lab practicals and a final thesis project. Many students publish before graduating.
