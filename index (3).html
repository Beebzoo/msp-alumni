About the MSP Alumni Network

The Maastricht Science Programme (MSP) has been educating future scientists and leaders since 2013. Our alumni network spans across 60+ countries, with graduates pursuing advanced degrees at the world's top universities and making their mark in diverse fields.

This interactive platform showcases the global reach of our alumni community, highlighting where our graduates are currently living and where they've pursued their Masters degrees.
