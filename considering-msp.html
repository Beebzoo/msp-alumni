In Their Own Words

What MSP graduates say about their journey

I realized that lab work wasn't for me. MSP gave me the freedom to discover that early on and the confidence to change direction without feeling limited by my degree.
MSP Graduate, 2023
Biology focus → MSc Intellectual Property Law → European Patent Office, Munich
MSP students develop skills that truly prepare them for the real world. The ability to communicate complex topics clearly remains one of the most valuable skills I bring to the workplace.
MSP Graduate, 2022
MSc International Development → Sustainability role at ALDI South headquarters
MSP's interdisciplinary foundation gave me exactly what I needed for my neuroscience PhD. The programme nurtured curiosity and encouraged independent thinking.
MSP Graduate, 2022
MSc Neurobiology, Amsterdam → PhD in Cancer Science at University of Oxford
I conducted research in Ecuador's ecological corridor and completed my thesis on crocodiles in Belize. Now I run my own conservation company leveraging technology to combat wildlife crime.
MSP Graduate, 2020
MSc Conservation Genetics, Copenhagen → Founder of Fauna Flux
The programme's educational approach was unlike anything I had experienced. It reignited my genuine enjoyment of learning and shaped me into the researcher I am today.
MSP Graduate, 2016
Nuclear Engineering scholarship, Singapore → Nuclear Researcher assessing reactor safety systems
When I started, I wasn't sure what I wanted to pursue. MSP's multidisciplinary approach was perfect for exploring different disciplines before committing to one path.
MSP Graduate, 2022
Chemistry focus → Family of international organizations → International Renewable Energy Agency
