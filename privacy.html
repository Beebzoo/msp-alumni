Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data in the MSP Alumni Network

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Your privacy is important to the Maastricht Science Programme (MSP) at Maastricht University. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data through the MSP Alumni Network website and related activities.

The MSP Alumni Network operates under the umbrella of Maastricht University's alumni engagement activities. For UM's general privacy practices, please refer to the UM General Privacy Statement.

Maastricht University

What This Website Displays

The MSP Alumni Network website displays aggregated, anonymized statistics only. We never publish individual names or personal details without explicit consent.

Data We Collect

From UM Student Records

  • Name and contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Date of birth and nationality
  • Educational information (programme, graduation date)

Data You Provide Directly

  • Current location
  • Career information (employer, job title)
  • Post-MSP education (Master's programme, university)
  • Communication preferences
  • LinkedIn profile (optional)

From Public Sources

As part of maintaining accurate alumni records, we may review publicly available professional information on LinkedIn to verify educational paths and track career outcomes. We always give you the option to opt out of this.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect financial information, health data (except dietary requirements for events), political opinions, religious beliefs, biometric data, or criminal records.

How We Manage Alumni Data

Purposes of Processing

  • Alumni engagement: Maintaining contact, sending newsletters, inviting to events
  • Statistical analysis: Understanding alumni outcomes to improve MSP and inform prospective students
  • Regional development: Providing anonymized data on graduate retention for stakeholders
  • Recruitment support: Sharing aggregate success stories with prospective students
  • Network facilitation: Enabling alumni-to-alumni connections (with consent)

Legal Basis

We process your personal data based on:

  • Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): Alumni engagement and institutional research are legitimate interests of Maastricht University.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR): For optional activities such as featuring your story publicly, participating in mentorship programmes, or joining WhatsApp groups.

Success Stories

We feature individual alumni stories only with explicit consent. Before publishing any story:

  1. We contact you with a specific request
  2. Story content is shared for your review
  3. We obtain your written approval (email confirmation is acceptable)
  4. You retain the right to withdraw consent at any time

If you withdraw consent, we will remove your story promptly from all platforms.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data we hold

Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

Erasure

Request deletion of your data (with some exceptions)

Restriction

Limit how we process your data

Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interest

Portability

Receive your data in a structured format

We will respond to all requests within 30 days.
